Caroline Wambui Ngure

Founder & Head Baker

Meet the Maker

Crafting Memories
One Cake at a Time

The journey of Nelcay Cakey Treats began in 2018 with a simple belief held by our founder, Caroline Wambui Ngure: that life’s most beautiful moments deserve a centerpiece that is as exquisite as the memory itself.

With a background rooted in the art of traditional baking and a contemporary eye for design, Caroline transformed her passion into a sanctuary of sweetness. For her, baking is not merely a process—it is a language of love, celebration, and meticulous artistry.

Every recipe developed at Nelcay is a testament to Caroline's dedication to perfection. From the precise fold of the batter to the delicate placement of a hand-sculpted sugar petal, her touch is present in every creation that leaves our studio.
